An international fintech group with offices across 7+ countries managed its org structure by hand: any staffing change — a transfer, a new role, a department reorg — meant half a day of HR work across systems and countries.

After the rollout, the company's structure became a single source that updates itself: a change is entered once and then rolls out across systems with no manual HR work. That saves half a day of specialist time per change — at the scale of an international group, that's a meaningful share of the HR department's payroll.
