## The gap this solution closes

Rulebooks and instructions live across dozens of documents. When an employee needs an exact answer, they either spend hours paging through volumes or interrupt a colleague who might remember where to look — and both lose working time.

## What the solution does

The AI assistant is trained on your internal documentation. You can ask a question in plain language and get an answer in seconds, always with a link to the source, so it can be checked rather than taken on faith.

## What's included and how we launch

The price covers loading and indexing the documentation, tuning the assistant to your workflow, and the launch — from $8,000. A subscription from $500/mo covers hosting and keeping the knowledge base updated as rules change. Launch takes 1–2 weeks. At a railway station, an assistant like this replaced manual search through volumes of technical documentation with an answer in seconds.
