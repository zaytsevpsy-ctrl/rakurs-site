## The leak this solution closes

A good share of prospects reach out in the evening or at night — while your sales team is asleep, the lead goes cold. By morning they've often messaged a competitor or simply changed their mind. Every one of those inquiries is lost revenue that never even shows up in your reports.

## What the assistant does

The AI assistant sits on your site and in your messengers and answers around the clock: it talks with the client, qualifies the lead against your criteria, books a meeting on its own, and hands a ready card to your CRM — the manager just shows up for the call.
