Station staff searched for the right clause in volumes of technical documentation by hand — finding a single answer could take tens of minutes.

We deployed a chat across all of the station's technical documentation: ask a question in plain language, get an answer in seconds, with a link to the source so it can be checked. Manual search through volumes of documentation stopped being part of the workday.
