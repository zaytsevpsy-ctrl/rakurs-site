Analysts at marketplace companies spent their working hours collecting and reconciling data from different sources — instead of drawing conclusions and proposing decisions, the team was assembling spreadsheets.

After the rollout, auto-analytics collects the data and produces recommendations daily, with no analyst involved. The team gets ready-made insights instead of raw spreadsheets, and spends its time thinking about decisions instead of reconciling data.
