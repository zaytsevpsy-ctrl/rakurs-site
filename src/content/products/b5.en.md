## The gap this solution closes

Sales looks at one system, finance at another, the warehouse at a third. While a leader pieces the picture together by hand, decisions get made on gut feeling instead of numbers.

## What the solution does

The panel brings every metric across the company and its departments onto one screen in real time: sales, finance, operations, marketing, people. AI flags anomalies on its own and points to which metric to look at first.

## What's included and how we launch

The price covers connecting your sources — CRM, accounting, inventory, ads, support — plus setting up the panel and alerts, from $9,500. A subscription from $1,200/mo covers hosting and retraining the alerts. Launch takes 3 weeks. Reports the team used to assemble by hand in spreadsheets now put themselves together after launch.
