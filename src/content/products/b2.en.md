## The leak this solution closes

As inquiry volume grows, the only way to keep up is hiring more operators — payroll grows with the flow of requests, not with revenue.

## What the solution does

AI intake sorts incoming requests: standard ones are handled end-to-end without a human, complex ones go to your team together with a ready breakdown — the person reviews the decision instead of building it from scratch. Integration runs through your existing CRM or ERP, so the AI works inside the systems your team already uses.

## What's included and how we launch

The price covers CRM/ERP integration, tuning the intake rules, and the launch — from $10,000. A subscription from $1,000/mo covers hosting, quality control, and retraining on new request types. Launch takes 2–3 weeks: it's assembled from a template already proven in production — at an insurance company this intake system delivered +30% processing speed while the operator headcount was reduced.
